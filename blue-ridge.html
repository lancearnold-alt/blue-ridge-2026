One Trip, Two Separate Parts

This is one proposed version of the trip — the most Blue Ridge time of any option.

First, Lance and Megan travel to Tuckasegee for Kaylee and Ben's wedding. After the wedding, the trip shifts into a separate mountain getaway with Sam and Jen. The two groups of friends are unrelated. Sam and Jen are joining the post-wedding getaway, not the wedding itself.

The wedding is fixed. The rest of this page is one proposed version of the trip. The Atlanta flights, meeting point, mountain base, trail, and return date can still change.

The Week at a Glance

Fly into Atlanta Friday, wedding Saturday, two mountain days with Sam & Jen, then home.

Proposed Schedule Proposed

One workable shape for the days — not locked in.

Friday — Fly In & Head UpSeptember 4
✈ Land ATL 10:00 AM · 🚗 rental pickup, then drive toward western NC

Land in Atlanta mid-morning, grab the rental, and drive up toward the wedding region. Check into wedding-area lodging and keep the evening light — a relaxed dinner, early night.

🛏 Lodging near Tuckasegee/Cashiers · Reserve Soon
Saturday — The WeddingSeptember 5
Daytime

Relaxed morning, nothing strenuous. Kaylee & Ben's wedding is at 3:14 PM in Tuckasegee.

This day is just Lance & Megan — Sam & Jen aren't part of the wedding. The getaway starts the next morning.
Sunday — Getaway Begins with Sam & JenSeptember 6
The shape of the day

Sam & Jen arrive and the getaway begins. Keep it to one waterfall, one scenic route, one walkable town, and a cabin dinner — unhurried, not a checklist. For example: meet around Cashiers or Sylva, walk to Silver Run Falls (or High Falls at Lake Glenville), take the Highlands–Cashiers Scenic Byway, wander a town, then cook in at the cabin.

🏡 Cabin base — Bear Creek Lake / Cashiers / Sylva (your pick below)
Monday — The Appalachian Trail DaySeptember 7 · Labor Day
Primary candidate

Siler Bald via the Appalachian Trail from Wayah Gap — a moderate climb to a grassy bald with wide views (a candidate, not committed).

Easier alternative

A shorter out-and-back section of the A.T. if the group wants something gentler.

Weather backup: if the forecast turns, swap to the Blue Ridge Parkway / Waterrock Knob overlooks or a waterfall-and-town day instead.
Tuesday — Return DaySeptember 8
✈ Flight home 6:10 PM from ATL · 🚗 return rental with a big buffer

Leave the mountains with a generous airport buffer, return the rental, and be at ATL well ahead of the 6:10 PM flight. No major Tuesday activity unless it's close to Atlanta.

Wednesday version: Tuesday becomes a true second getaway day in the mountains — this is the preferred way to fit a full A.T. hike without rushing.
Wednesday — Return Day (extended)September 9
✈ Return flight (changeable to Wed) · 🚗 unhurried drive back to ATL

With the extension, the return shifts to Wednesday — a relaxed drive back to Atlanta and the flight home, no rush after two full getaway days.
