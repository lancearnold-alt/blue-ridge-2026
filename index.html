Most mountain time
Western North Carolina

Base in the mountains right by the wedding — around Cashiers and Sylva. Sam & Jen drive in for a two-day getaway before or after the wedding: a waterfall, a scenic drive, a small town, and a cabin Airbnb.

About the area
The southern Blue Ridge — cool, green, full of water. Cashiers and Highlands are small mountain crossroads; Sylva is a laid-back downtown with breweries and coffee; Tuckasegee (the wedding) sits on a river near Bear Creek Lake. Waterfalls everywhere and the Appalachian Trail within reach.

Closer to the airport
North Georgia Mountains

Get together in the North Georgia mountains — much closer to Atlanta and to Lexington. Gorges, waterfalls, and quirky towns, with the wedding a separate drive on its own days.

About the area
The North Georgia mountains, ~1.5–2 hrs from Atlanta. Helen is a Bavarian-themed river village; Dahlonega is a gold-rush town that's now wine country; Tallulah Gorge is a dramatic 1,000-ft canyon; and Amicalola Falls (729 ft) is the tallest in the Southeast.
Things to do
Gorge · hike

Tallulah Gorge

Rim trails and a swinging suspension bridge over the canyon.

Waterfall · tallest

Amicalola Falls

729 ft — the highest in the Southeast — with viewing decks.

Town · fun

Helen

Bavarian village: German food, river tubing, an alpine coaster.

Town · wine

Dahlonega

Historic square, gold museum, and wine tasting rooms.

Simplest logistics
Around Atlanta

Get together right in Atlanta — food, walkable neighborhoods, big attractions, and an easy day trip. The wedding is a separate overnight run to the mountains.

About the area
Atlanta is green and best explored in neighborhoods, tied together by the BeltLine — a 22-mile trail (old rail lines) lined with murals, breweries, and food halls. Midtown has the parks and museums; Decatur is a walkable square just east. Easy and weather-proof.
Things to do
Walk / bike

Atlanta BeltLine

A trail loop with murals and public art — the best way to see the city.

Food + rooftop

Ponce City Market

Landmark food hall with a rooftop of games and skyline views.

Attraction

Georgia Aquarium

One of the world's largest — whale sharks and manta rays.

History

MLK Historical Park

Dr. King's birth home and church, plus the civil-rights museum.

Optional day trip

North Georgia nature day

Tallulah Gorge or Amicalola Falls are ~1.5–2 hrs away — an easy add.
